Our Courses

At Sw. Narendra Singh Mahavidyalaya, we offer quality education through well-structured undergraduate programs that prepare students for academic excellence and professional success.

Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)

The BA program provides a strong foundation in humanities and social sciences. Students can specialize in subjects such as Hindi, English, History, Political Science, and more.

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 Passed (Any Stream)
  • Career Options: Civil Services, Journalism, Teaching, Social Work

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)

The B.Sc program focuses on building strong analytical and problem-solving skills in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology.

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 Passed (Science Stream)
  • Career Options: Research, IT Sector, Laboratory Technician, Higher Studies (M.Sc, MCA)

Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.)

The B.Ed program prepares students to become professional teachers with modern teaching methodologies, pedagogy, and classroom management skills.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: Graduation with minimum 50% marks
  • Career Options: Teaching in Schools, Coaching Institutes, Education Sector Jobs
