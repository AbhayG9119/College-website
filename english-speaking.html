About the Course

This English Speaking Course is designed to help learners develop strong communication skills, improve fluency, and gain confidence in both professional and everyday conversations. The course covers grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, and practical usage of the language.

Course Highlights

  • Improve fluency and pronunciation
  • Learn professional vocabulary
  • Interactive speaking practice sessions
  • Group discussions and role plays
  • Business communication skills
  • Confidence building activities

Who Should Join?

This course is ideal for students, professionals, job seekers, and anyone who wants to enhance their English communication skills for personal or professional growth.

Duration & Mode

  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Mode: Online / Offline
  • Flexible timings available
